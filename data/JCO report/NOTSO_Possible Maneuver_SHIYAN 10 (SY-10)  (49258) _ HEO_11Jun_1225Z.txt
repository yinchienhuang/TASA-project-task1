Mission Summary:
Cosmos 2589 (NORAD ID: 64467) is a classified Russian military communications and defense technology satellite designed and manufactured by Reshetnev ISS. The satellite—internally designated under the 14F166 / 14F166A project framework—was initially deployed into a highly elliptical, super-synchronous transfer orbit. Over the following weeks, Cosmos 2589 utilized its onboard electric maneuvering thrusters (SPD-100V) alongside a co-launched subsatellite (Cosmos 2590) to circularize its orbit, eventually settling into its planned operational position within geostationary orbit (GEO) at approximately 98°E longitude to support strategic independent communications for the Russian Aerospace Forces.
