Mission Summary:
SJ-29A is a Chinese space target detection technology demonstration satellite manufactured by the Shanghai Academy of Spaceflight Technology (SAST/CASC), launched 31 December 2025 from Wenchang aboard a Chang Zheng 7A into geostationary transfer orbit. Estimated at approximately 3,500 kg, SJ-29A was co-launched with SJ-29B (67403) (manufacturer: CAS Microsat) as a dual-satellite configuration assessed for coordinated space object surveillance, potentially carrying optical, infrared, or radar sensor payloads.
