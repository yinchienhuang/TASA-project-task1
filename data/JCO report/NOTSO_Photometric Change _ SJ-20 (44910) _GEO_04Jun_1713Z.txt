Mission Summary:
Shijian 20 (SJ-20) is a heavy Chinese experimental communications satellite operating in geostationary orbit (GEO). Serving as the maiden verification flight for China’s next-generation DFH-5 satellite platform, its primary mission is to test ultra-high-performance technologies, including high-thrust ion propulsion, laser communications, and a massive solar wing configuration, providing up to 28 kilowatts of payload power. By successfully validating these advanced systems after the failure of the previous DFH-5 prototype (Shijian 18) in 2017, Shijian 20 laid the structural and technological foundation for China’s modern, high-capacity civilian and military space communications infrastructure.
